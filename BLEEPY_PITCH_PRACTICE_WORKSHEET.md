## üé§ **Common Issues & Solutions**

### **Issue: Speaking too fast**
- **Solution:** Practice with pauses. Breathe between sentences.

### **Issue: Not emphasizing "Bleepy"**
- **Solution:** Say "Bleepy" slightly slower and louder. Pause briefly after it.

### **Issue: Running over 60 seconds**
- **Solution:** Cut less essential details. Focus on core message.

### **Issue: Sounding robotic**
- **Solution:** Practice as if telling a friend. Be conversational.

### **Issue: Forgetting key points**
- **Solution:** Use cue card. Practice section by section, then combine.

## üéØ **Final Filming Strategy**

**Take 1:** Warm-up run-through (don't stress about perfection)  
**Take 2:** Focus on timing (ensure 60 seconds)  
**Take 3:** Focus on delivery (energetic, confident)  
**Take 4:** Best of everything (timing + delivery)  
**Take 5:** Backup option (in case Take 4 has issues)

**Pick the best take, or edit together best segments!**

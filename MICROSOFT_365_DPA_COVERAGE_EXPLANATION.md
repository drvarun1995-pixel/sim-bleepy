# Microsoft 365 DPA - Exchange Online Coverage Explanation

## ✅ This is Normal and Expected!

**Important:** Microsoft's Data Protection Addendum (DPA) does **NOT** explicitly list every service like "Exchange Online" or "Microsoft Graph API". This is by design and is **completely normal**.

## How Microsoft's DPA Works

### 1. **General Coverage Model**

Microsoft's DPA uses a **general coverage model** that applies to:
- ✅ **All Microsoft Online Services**
- ✅ **All Core Online Services** 
- ✅ **All Products and Services** covered by the Microsoft Online Services Terms (OST)

### 2. **What to Look For in Your DPA**

Instead of looking for "Exchange Online", look for these terms in your document:

#### Key Terms to Find:

1. **"Microsoft Online Services"** or **"Online Services"**
   - This umbrella term includes Exchange Online

2. **"Core Online Services"**
   - Exchange Online is a Core Online Service

3. **"Products and Services"**
   - Exchange Online is included in this category

4. **"Customer Data"** or **"Personal Data"**
   - The DPA covers all customer data processed by Microsoft services

5. **"Data Processing"** or **"Data Controller/Processor"**
   - These sections apply to all services, including Exchange Online

## ğŸ’¡ **Delivery Tips**

### **Pacing (60 seconds total):**
- **0:00-0:08:** Personal Introduction & Hook
- **0:08-0:18:** Problem Statement
- **0:18-0:35:** Solution & Target Users (Students & Educators)
- **0:35-0:48:** Competitive Advantage & Traction

### **Voice & Delivery:**
- **Start with energy:** Capture attention immediately
- **Speak clearly:** Articulate each word, especially "Bleepy"
- **Vary pace:** Slow down for key points (Bleepy, impact, CTA)
- **Show passion:** Your voice should convey excitement about solving this problem
- **Smile naturally:** Visual warmth increases engagement

### **Body Language (if on camera):**
- **Eye contact:** Look directly at camera lens (not screen)
- **Confident posture:** Sit or stand straight, shoulders back
- **Natural gestures:** Use hands to emphasize points naturally
- **Facial expression:** Show enthusiasm, especially when discussing impact

### **Background & Setting:**
- **Clean background:** Professional or medical setting
- **Good lighting:** Face well-lit, avoid harsh shadows
- **Quiet environment:** Minimize background noise
- **Stable camera:** Use tripod or steady surface
